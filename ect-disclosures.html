3. How to Complete a Purchase

The following steps are required to purchase credits or a subscription on MyReportComments:

  1. Visit myreportcomments.com and create a free account using your email address
  2. Navigate to the Pricing section and select the credit pack or plan you wish to purchase
  3. Review the order summary, including the total price in ZAR inclusive of VAT
  4. Click "Buy now" — you will be redirected to a secure Stripe checkout page
  5. Enter your payment details on the Stripe checkout page
  6. Review your order one final time and click "Pay" to confirm
  7. You will receive an on-screen confirmation and a receipt by email
  8. Credits are added to your account immediately upon successful payment
You have the opportunity to review and correct your order at step 6 before payment is finalised. Do not click "Pay" until you are satisfied with your order.

4. Payment and Security

All payments are processed by Stripe, a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment processor. MyReportComments does not store, access, or transmit your card number, CVV, or any banking credentials.

Security measures in place:

  • All pages served over HTTPS (TLS encryption)
  • Stripe handles all card data — we receive only a transaction confirmation
  • 3D Secure authentication applied where required by your bank
  • Fraud detection via Stripe Radar

5. Refunds and Cancellations

In accordance with Section 44 of the ECT Act and the Consumer Protection Act 68 of 2008:

  • Credit packs: Full refund available within 7 days of purchase, provided fewer than 5 credits have been used
  • Pro Annual: Full refund available within 14 days of purchase, provided fewer than 20 comments have been generated
  • Processing time: Approved refunds processed within 5–10 business days to your original payment method

To request a refund, email hello@myreportcomments.com with your order reference. Full details are in our Refund Policy.

6. Dispute Resolution

If you have a complaint or dispute:

  1. Contact us first — email hello@myreportcomments.com. We aim to respond within 1 business day and resolve disputes within 10 business days.
  2. If unresolved, you may escalate to the National Consumer Commission at thencc.org.za
  3. For POPIA-related complaints: Information Regulator of South Africa at www.justice.gov.za/inforeg
  4. For payment disputes, contact your bank or card issuer directly

7. Transaction Records

A record of your transactions is available in your account dashboard. You may request a copy of your full transaction history by emailing hello@myreportcomments.com. Records are retained for a minimum of 5 years as required by SARS.

8. Technical Requirements

MyReportComments is accessible via any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge — version released within the last 2 years) on desktop or mobile. No software installation is required for the hosted web app. The free HTML tool is a standalone file that runs entirely offline in your browser after the initial download.
